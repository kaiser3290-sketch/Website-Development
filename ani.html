About the Series

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family was slaughtered by demons. With his sister Nezuko turned into a demon, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to find a cure and protect humanity.

Main Characters

  • Tanjiro Kamado - The protagonist and swordsman
  • Nezuko Kamado - Tanjiro's demon sister
  • Inosuke Hashibira - Wild Demon Slayer
  • Zenitsu Agatsuma - Thunder Breathing user
